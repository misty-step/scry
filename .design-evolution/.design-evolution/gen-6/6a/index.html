Nodes increment their term and transition to candidate state, requesting votes from other nodes.
\\n

\\n

Explanation
\\n
\\n When a leader election times out in Raft, followers increment their current term, \\n transition to the candidate state, and send RequestVote RPCs to other nodes. \\n This mechanism ensures that the cluster can recover from leader failures without \\n external intervention. The candidate must receive votes from a majority of nodes \\n to become the new leader.\\n
